How do I create an account?
Click on "Sign Up" in the header, fill out your details, and submit. You'll get a confirmation email to verify your account.
How do I apply for a job?
Browse the "Jobs" page, select a job, then click "Apply Now" on the job detail page to submit your application.
How do I post a job?
After signing in as a client, go to your Dashboard and click "Post a New Job", fill in the form, and publish.
Who can I contact for support?
Use the "Contact" page to send us a message, or email support@workathomewithshawn.com.
